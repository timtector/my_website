About Us

Golf Review was made to give golfers around the world the place to view and review golf courses in our Country. We make it easy for you to view the courses that you are interested in so that you can make the right decision on your golfing endevours.

Why Trust Us

We know what golfers want. As golfers we want to know what the course, clubhouse and facilities are like. We dont want to be paying crazy money if it not really worth it, so we give honest reviews. We are not affiliated with any golf clubs in Ireland. We judge them how we see them and so do people who review on this website.
